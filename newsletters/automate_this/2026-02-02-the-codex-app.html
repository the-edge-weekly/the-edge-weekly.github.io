What is OpenAI Codex?

Let me break this down in simple terms. OpenAI Codex is like having a brilliant coding partner. One who never gets tired, never makes typos, and has memorized every programming tutorial ever written.

I've been watching the automation space for years. I can tell you that this represents a major shift in how we approach technical tasks.
