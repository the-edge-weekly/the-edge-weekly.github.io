It was Good Boss. Thank You Boss

The 3 traits that separate great crypto mentors from the rest

Finding the Right Mentors in Web3

In blockchain development, your career can change in a single sprint. After 3+ years building on Solana, I've learned the gap between struggling alone and fast growth comes down to one thing: finding the right people to learn from.

What Makes a Great Crypto Mentor?

Most developers think mastering Rust or PDAs is everything. They're wrong. The mentors who actually move your career forward share 3 key traits:

  1. They've shipped production code - Not just tutorials, actual protocols
  2. They share war stories - The edge cases, the 3am bug fixes
  3. They challenge your assumptions - They push back, not just validate

Where to Find Them

  • Solana Discord developer channels
  • Twitter/X crypto dev communities
  • Open source project contributors
