Finding the Right Mentors in Web3

In blockchain development, your career can change in a single sprint. After 3+ years building on Solana, I've learned the gap between struggling alone and fast growth comes down to one thing: finding the right people to learn from.

What Makes a Great Crypto Mentor?

Most developers think mastering Rust or PDAs is everything. They're wrong. The mentors who actually move your career forward share 3 key traits that go way beyond technical skills:

  1. They've shipped production code - Not just tutorials, actual protocols handling real money
  2. They share war stories - The edge cases, the 3am bug fixes, the security scares
  3. They challenge your assumptions - Great mentors don't just validate, they push back

Where to Find Them

  • Solana Discord developer channels
  • Twitter/X crypto dev communities
  • Open source project contributors
  • Hackathon teams and judges
