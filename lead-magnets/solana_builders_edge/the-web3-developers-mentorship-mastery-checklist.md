Use this to accelerate your career growth and avoid common pitfalls that keep developers stuck.

## Next Steps

Start by choosing 3-5 items from this checklist that align with your current situation and skill level. Focus on building genuine relationships and providing value first. Remember: great mentorship relationships are built over time through consistent, valuable interactions, not single conversations.

The crypto industry moves fast, but strong professional relationships are what turn individual sprints into sustainable career growth.
